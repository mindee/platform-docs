# OCR

This utility is designed to allow you automatically extract the raw text from each page of a document.&#x20;

## Setup



1. To create a OCR utility, you need to click on **Models**, and then on **Create your document AI model**.
2. Under the **Document Utilities** section, click on **OCR.**
3. You can now use the **Live Test** tab to process documents.<br>
